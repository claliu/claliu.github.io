### on the contrary

If it is not true,
let Mr Darcy contradict it himself.

Till he does, I hope never to encounter him.

Poor, unfortunate Mr Wickham.

On the contrary,
Wickham is twice the man Darcy is.

And let us hope, a rather more willing dancer.
